CABINET PAINTING

What to know about the color for kitchen cabinets

Kitchen cabinets are either painted with acrylic paint, oil paint, or spray paint. Acrylic paint is the most preferred among them. The most important feature of this paint is that it does not smell and dries quickly. As a company, we offer the highest quality paints and craftsmanship. You choose the color and we renovate your cabinets so they are like brand new.

How many coats are used to paint kitchen cabinets?

If you want to use your kitchen cabinet for a long time and want it to fully reflect its color, it is sufficient to apply two coats of paint. We extend the life of your cabinets by using the best quality paints so that you can use them for many years. If your cabinets are wooden cabinets, you must use acrylic paint.

If your cabinets are not wooden, we recommend oil paint. By using oil paint, you can have brand new cabinets and add elegance to your elegance.

If your cabinet legs are metal, the paint you use will be different, of course. Acrylic paint is used for metals. For this reason, you should use an oil-based primer that is compatible with acrylic paint.

Spray paints are used to obtain a smooth and even surface on wooden surfaces and wooden furniture. Spray paints are preferred more than other colors. The main reason is that it is easier to implement and cleaner. When painting, paint can drip from the brush and brush marks can be left behind. Spray paint is more preferred as it prevents these negatives. Spray paint can give a cleaner, flawless, pure and aesthetic look.

How should lacquered cabinets be cleaned?

Once the painting process is complete, the cleaning phase begins. You can trust our teammates to be meticulous in this regard. Our masters do a very clean and tidy job without leaving you with too much work. Their job is to simply clean it with the help of a microfibre cloth. You can easily remove the paint residue by washing the microfibre cloth with soap.
