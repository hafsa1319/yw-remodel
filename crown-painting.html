What is Stryropor, how is it used?

Recently, this product has been preferred a lot in places. The most important reason for this is that the product is light and easy to use. As is known, this product is obtained from foam. It does not pollute the area it is applied to like drywall, and it is more preferred because it is easier to paint than drywall.

Drywalls are not painted immediately after they are applied to the place. But the styrofoam can be painted immediately after application. It is more preferred because it is very easy to disassemble the product from where it was made.

How to Paint a styrofoam?

Painting the styrofoam is carried out in two stages. These are assembly and painting. First, the product is mounted on the desired area. After the assembly process is completed, the painting phase begins. The point to be considered here is to decide which paint to use. There are many types of paints, all you have to do is to decide the paint according to your desire. But the preferred and recommended paint is plastic paint. Painting process:

First of all, if the area you are going to paint is furnished, they should be removed from the room or covered with nylon tarpaulin. Make fik the tarps with masking tape. After the preparation of the environment is finished, you need to thin the paint in a clean and wide bowl and achieve its consistency.

After that, the first coat of paint is applied with the help of a brush. After the painting process is finished, wait to dry. After it dries, it remains to do the checks and clean the excess with sandpaper. After the second coat of paint of the place you dusted and cleaned is painted, the process is finished.

FAQ

What is stryrofoam?

Styrofoam is a synthetic material and is made from petroleum. It is first refined and shaped into small balls. As a result of the release of steam to the inner parts of these balls, the balls swell and stick together. Styrofoam is used for insulation or packaging.

What are the prices for styrofoam?

Prices for styrofoam may vary. Styrofoam prices vary depending on durability, place of use and model. It is necessary to compare prices and choose the most durable styrofoam according to the model. To buy Styrofoam at cheaper prices, discount periods need to be followed.

Should Gypsum or Styrofoam be preferred?

Generally, gypsum plasterboard is used in ceiling or wall decorations. But as an alternative to plaster, styrofoam performs the same task. Unlike gypsum plasterboard, styrofoam is made of foam and is easier to work with. Although plaster is a difficult process, it is used for many years compared to styrofoam.

With what does Styrofoam stick? What needs to be considered?

There are special adhesives for bonding styrofoams. If this is not possible, the styrofoams can also be bonded with satin plaster. The most durable styrofoam application is with a plaster layer filling. Plaster is recommended for gluing styrofoam.

What is Styrofoam glued with?

It is possible to bond Styrofoam with Styrofoam adhesives. It is possible for the foam to adhere to the foam during the process or for the foam to adhere directly to the concrete. With the adhesive, the foam can even be glued to wood or metal. As an alternative to special styrofoam adhesives, plaster or silicone can also be used.

What does a suspended ceiling cost?

Prices per square metre are quoted for ceiling work. However, the prices for suspended ceilings are not fixed, as one needs to know the type of material to be used in the suspended ceiling. The prices for suspended ceilings vary depending on the material used. You can contact us to get the best price.

What are the wall battens glued with?

For gluing the wall battens, quick-setting adhesives are preferable. Wooden battens adhere quickly with spray adhesive. However, for all other wall battens to adhere quickly, it is necessary to use styrofoam adhesive. For this, the wall must be absolutely clean.
