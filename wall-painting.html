It is fairly important that the decoration of your home or the environment you are in is compatible and does not tire the eyes. The colors of your wall play a huge part in this compatibility. The walls of your home or your work areas may lose their harmony with the furniture and other objects you have renewed over time. Or it can lose its color, texture and aesthetics over time. It is precisely in this situation that we transform the atmosphere and environment into completely different spaces by offering you beautiful colors.

Painting requires skill. It needs effort and above all experience. We are happy to serve you with the competent masters and experience of our company. We offer you the most beautiful colors and change your environment by guaranteeing a clean finish.

What to do before you start painting?

Although painting the walls seems to be a situation that only concerns the wall, some preparations must be made in advance. To begin with, if the area to be painted is furnished, this furniture must be safely protected from paint. This way, the painting process will be completed without paint stains. In addition, objects such as carpets and rugs are put away.

When painting the wall, be careful not to paint the sockets and mirrors. Using nylon or strips for protection will prevent painting here. Masking tape helps a lot. When painting the ceiling, pay attention to the chandeliers and complete the process without paint.

Before painting the wall, check which paint the wall is suitable for. The rough spots or swollen areas on the wall should be corrected first. Doing this in advance will make your work easier and ensure longer use of the paint.

What materials are used when painting walls?

Sufficient paint for painting walls and rollers and thin brushes for painting. You will also need a large paint container. The brush should touch the paint lightly.

Masking tape makes your work much easier and practical. It makes it easy to clean wall-mounted sockets and switches that tire you out when they are painted and protects the parts from paint.

Primer ensures better adhesion of the paint to the wall. It protects the paint from external influences. Thanks to the primer, you extend the life of the painted surface.

Paint roller is the tool you use to apply the paint to the wall. When painting walls, you can choose between short-handled or long-handled rollers.

Brushes are used when painting narrow and thin areas that cannot be painted with a roller.

How is a wall painted?

After all precautions have been taken and the environment has been prepared, the painting process begins. To make the paint more durable, a primer is applied to the wall beforehand. This process prolongs the life of the paint. After the primer has dried, the paint is applied to the wall.

Brushstrokes are executed naively with light movements from bottom to top. With horizontal movements of the roller, the paint runs in stripes. After the brushing process of the first layer is completed, the second layer is passed through.
