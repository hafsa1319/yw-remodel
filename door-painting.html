Door Painting

How to paint which door?

Many areas come to mind about home decoration.An important part is the doors. For a stylish and modern look of your home, your doors should be clean and painted. You will be able to reach what should be done here step by step. Painting old interior doors will provide a newer look with less expense than replacing them. It is possible to provide a newer look with small budgets.what you need to do is to determine which door is your door is correctly. Because every paint is not suitable for every door.

Techniques of Painting ƒ∞nterior Doors.

If you want to paint the interior doors of your house or the place where you are, the first thing you should do is to find out what kind of your door is. Then, all you need to do is to supply the materials. As for, painting techniques , there are three different tehniques. These are the usage of rollers and brushes, the usage of pressure guns and dipping method. While painting your door, if there is any decoration on the door, it must be covered first. The most tiring and time-consuming painting technique among these painting techniques is painting brush and roller.

Door painting with a pressure gun will provide you to gain both time and money. An important point here is that there should be a certain distance between the door to be painted and gun.If you can not maket his adjustment well, it will cause the paint to go more to certain areas and form a thick layer.

The Dipping Method should be done by professional workers. This method is more difficult and more expensive than the other methods. So it would be better to get help from an expert.

Door Painting Tips

Things to do before painting interior doors:

-To sand of the door surface
-To Wet the door surface

Painting Techniques for Wooden Interior Doors

The most challenging of all door types is the wooden door. It is more difficult and troublesome to paint. Because when painting wooden doors, both special materials are required and varnish and varnish conditions are need to be controlled. Wooden door painting stages:

-To select a suitable paint fort he door
- If there is previous polish, to clean it
-To remove varnish if any
-Transition to the painting process

If the door is to be painted in a lighter color than its old color, several layers of paint must be applied for this. Thus, the old color will be completely covered and it will gain its new appearance.

Painting American Doors

The most preferred door type in interiors is the American door. Painting these doors is very easy and fast. American doors can be easily painted in the desired color. The most important thing to consider here will be the choice of paint. It is recommended to paint these doors with water-based paints. Water-based paints are odorless and do not discolor or turn yellow in long-term use.

Painting Methods for Lacquered Doors

Lacquered doors require a special work that other doors do not have. When painting such doors, it must be a wood door paint with cellulosic properties. These paints with cellulosic content will make your door look newer, more aesthetic and smooth.

In addition to this, a paint gun should be preferred in the painting process of lacquered doors. It is much more difficult to achieve the same aesthetic and beautiful appearance by using a roller or brush.

Painting Process on decorated doors

In self-patterned interior doors, the painting process is more time-consuming. When painting such doors, it is necessary to be more meticulous so that it does not lose its old feature and texture. When painting such doors, both brush, roller and paint gun techniques are used.

Decorative doors are painted in two stages. At the first stage, the ornaments and decorations on the door should be covered with a tape or newspaper. After painting the other parts of the door and the paint dries, these tapes are removed. The second stage is painting the places that were previously covered with tape at this time. The painted area is covered and the same process is carried out on the ornaments. Thus, both areas are beautifully painted and have a new look.

Light Color Interior Door Paintings

If the door you want to paint is light colored, painting is much easier. When painting doors in white or cream tones, priming is not required before painting. What you need to do is to clean the doors well before painting. Your paint will look much nicer and smoother on a clean door.

FAQ

How much paint is enough for a normal door?

About 1 kilo of paint goes to a normal door. However, depending on whether the paint is polished and what kind of door the door is, the amount of paint changes.

What Are Doors Painted With?

Water-based paints are preferred for painting doors. If it is a varnished door, it should be sanded first and then painted.

What Should Be Considered While Painting Doors?

When painting the door, it is necessary to pay attention to the fact that the paint is suitable for the door.

How Many Doors Can Be Painted With 3 kg of Paint?

With 3 kilos of paint, an application takes place between 7 and 9 doors. Depending on how much the paints are diluted and the type of paint, their use varies. In addition, the number of doors also changes, as the presence of varnish on the doors will change the paint suction speed.

How Much Paint Goes to the Panel Door?

If there is a light color paint on the panel doors, it is sufficient to paint 2 coats. However, if the panel door is painted with a dark color paint, at least 3 layers of paint are required for the new color.

How is Panel Door Paint Thinned?

1/10 of water is used to thin the panel door paint. In order to thin the paint of panel doors, it is necessary to put water in the paint in proportion and mix it. At the same time, it is necessary to wait 3-4 hours for panel paints to dry between coats.

How to Paint Which Door?

Painting wooden doors is one of the most difficult processes. Because the paint of wooden doors is special, they also need to be painted with varnish and varnish after painting. For wooden doors, it is necessary to purchase wooden door paints sold in hardware stores. Painting American doors is quite easy and fast. American doors are painted with water-based paints with a brush or roller brush. In order to paint the lacquered doors, first of all the varnish should be removed and then the painting should be done with the desired technique. Lacquered doors are doors that require more care than other doors. Cellulosic wood door paints should be preferred for painting lacquered doors. In this way, a smoother image is obtained.
